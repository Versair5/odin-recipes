Recipe for CHOCOLATE MOUSSE

This is a simple chocolate mousse recipe from my father.

This dessert is really easy to make and perfect for a quick sweet fix.

It doesnt need a lot of ingredients therefore it is no cost to make. It needs to be chilled a few hours before servng though.

Ingredients:

  • Milk chocolate
  • Dark chocolate
  • 2 eggs
  • Whipped cream
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. Heat chocoalte in a double boiler until melted, then cool.
  2. Separate your eggs into whites and yolks.
  3. Whip the yolks with the sugar and whip the whites with a pinch of salt.
  4. Combine cool chocolate with egg yolk mixture.
  5. Fold the whites in the chocolate mixture.
  6. Add the batter in glasses and cover with cling film. Cool for 3 hours in the fridge.
  7. Serve with whipped cream.
