Recipe for LASAGNA

This is a simple lasagna recipe from my grandmother.

This lasagna is really easy to make and perfect for a weeknight dinner.

It doesnt need a lot of ingredients therefore it is no cost to make. It does however take time to cook in the oven.

Ingredients:

  • Sheet pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Basil
  • A lot of cheese
  • 2 packs of ham
  • Bolognese sauce (Preferably Homemade)

Steps:

  1. Prepare bolognese sauce.
  2. Grate cheese.
  3. Chop basil.
  4. boil sheet pasta.
  5. Grease the baking tray in which you will be preparing your lasagna.
  6. Make a bechamel sauce.
  7. Start layering. Start with the bechamel, add pasta, add bolognese and tomato sauce, add ham and grated cheese.
  8. Repeat step 7 until you fill the tray.
  9. Bake at 180 degrees celsius for 45 minutes and serve.
