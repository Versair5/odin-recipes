Recipe for MAC AND CHEESE

Back to Recipes

This is a simple mac and cheese recipe from my mother.

This mac and cheese is really easy to make and perfect for a weeknight dinner.
It doesnt need a lot of ingredients therefore it is no cost to make.
It does however take time to cook in the oven.

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni
  • Grated parmesan
  • Grated pecorino
  • Grated gouda
  • Grated Cheddar
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Heat butter and combine with 1 teaspoon flour. Mix well.
  2. Add 2 cups of milk to flour and butter mix. Whisk constantly.
  3. Add milk until sauce is to your thickness.
  4. Boil your pasta.
  5. Add your cheese to milk sauce.
  6. Add macaroni to cheese sauce.
  7. Serve.
